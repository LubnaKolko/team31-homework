Tacos recipe 
Ingredients:
6 small taco shells
250g ground beef or chicken
1 tablespoon taco seasoning
1 tablespoon olive oil
1/2 cup shredded lettuce
1/2 cup diced tomatoes
1/4 cup shredded cheese
1/4 cup sour cream
Optional: salsa or guacamole
Instructions:
Cook the Meat:

Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat.
Add ground beef or chicken and cook until browned (5-7 minutes).
Stir in taco seasoning and a splash of water. Simmer for 2-3 minutes.
Prepare the Toppings:

Chop the lettuce and dice the tomatoes.
Set out shredded cheese, sour cream, and optional salsa or guacamole.
Assemble the Tacos:

Warm the taco shells in the oven or microwave.
Fill each shell with cooked meat, then add lettuce, tomatoes, cheese, and sour cream.
Serve:

Serve immediately with your favorite toppings or sides!
